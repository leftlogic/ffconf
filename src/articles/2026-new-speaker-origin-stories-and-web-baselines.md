## From Our Archive

FFConf started in 2009, but did you know that it started in the pub on a snowy day in Brighton. It took only nine months from a random conversation over a beer to become the conference we all know and love.

The full story includes a photo of the original event plan scribbles, so instead here's the snowman we both made hours before FFConf was conceived:

![A particularly young looking Remy besides the head-heavy snowman, whom randomly smokes a cigar](https://assets.ffconf.org/img/2026/07/49xNJbynbL-pre-ffconf-snowman.jpg)

Julie writes:

> Remy was complaining about the lack of a conference in the UK that talked about things he was interested in […] So I grabbed a (paper) pub menu and together we sketched out the idea – venue, speakers, possible dates, budget, sponsorship, marketing, website.

So much has changed over those 17 years but our passion to bring people together and share a special atmosphere and connection remains strong.

[Read the full story here](https://ffconf.org/articles/2025-the-origin-story-of-ffconf-by-julie/)

## Community News

If you follow Remy on Bluesky or Mastodon, then you'll have seen him posting about discovering web features that have been stable and widely supported, such as `#private` properties in JavaScript.

[Baseline](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Glossary/Baseline/Compatibility) is a superb way to help decide what's worth using, but _also_ to find out what's new.

- Every feature and its baseline support on [webstatus.dev](https://webstatus.dev/) - which you can [filtered for just this year](https://webstatus.dev/?q=baseline_date%3A2026-01-01..2026-07-01&columns=name%2Cbaseline_status%2Cavailability_chrome%2Cavailability_edge%2Cavailability_firefox%2Cavailability_safari%2Cstable_chrome%2Cstable_edge%2Cstable_firefox%2Cstable_safari&column_options=baseline_status_high_date)
- Maybe you want [alerts for when a feature lands](https://web.dev/blog/baseline-alerts) in baseline?
- Or you prefer "stats for nerds" or want to see [which browsers are failing](https://wpt.fyi/results/?label=experimental&label=master&aligned)
