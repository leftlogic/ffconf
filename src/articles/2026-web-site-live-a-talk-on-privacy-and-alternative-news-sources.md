## From Our Archive

We welcomed a fellow Brightonian and friend of FFConf, Chetan Padia to the stage last year to ask, are we ["Powerless by Design?"](https://ffconf.org/talks/2025_chetan/)

Chetan explores the tools that we use as default, raises the question of monopolies, convinces us that we are influencers, and gives us a comprehensive kit of alternative options that prioritise privacy, open standards, and have a clear business model.

His call to action is: show support by donating to open source and freemium models, keep talking about these issues, try something new, and stay curious.

## Community News

Chetan mentions many alternative sources for services, products and news  ([EU Alternatives](https://european-alternatives.eu/) is an excellent start for services), but we wanted to signpost his suggestions for news rather than relying on social media as your source:

- [Kagi News](https://news.kagi.com/) - updated once a day, perfect antidote to doom scrolling
- [Fix the News](https://fixthenews.com/) - journalism focused on _positive_ news around the world
- [The Nerve](https://www.thenerve.news/) - culture, politics and tech founded by Carole Cadwalladr and others (of the Cambridge Analytica exposé)
- [Ground News](https://ground.news/) - a source that lets you compare _both_ sides of the stories
