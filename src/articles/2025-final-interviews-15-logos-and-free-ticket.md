## From Our Archive

Fifteen events has also seen 15 event designs and logos. A conscious design choice by Remy from the very start has enabled us to grow and change over the years.

FFConf in 2025 is an event which chooses people over technology, that aims to inspire and motivate, and that champions [a better web](https://ffconf.org/core-principles/).
