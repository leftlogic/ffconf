## Latest Event News

Our [inclusion scholarship](https://ffconf.org/scholarship/) is now open to anyone from an under-represented group in tech. Apply now to receive a free ticket to attend, with limited funds available to cover travel.

Gwen attended last year without knowing anyone so we paired her with a buddy so she had a friendly face from the start. She says ["the sense of community and friendliness was incredible"](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/gwen-michailidis_ffconf-ffconf-womenintech-activity-7395478136542625792-fLkN/)

We are looking for companies to support the scheme so please hit reply if this is something you can help with.

## From Our Archive

A previous scholarship recipient is Charlotte Dann who spoke in 2019. She says:

> if it wasn't for FFConf's generosity and commitment to creating a diverse community, I wouldn't be the developer I am today

Check out Charlotte's fascinating [talk](https://ffconf.org/talks/taking-the-web-off-the-screen/) about the rise of generative art (pre-AI, remember those heady days?!) and gives us an insight into laser cutters, pen plotters, and 3D printing.

> This is why I love generative coding so much because you get to be super creative and expressive making generative art

## Community News

This last week, it's been a slew of Standard.site information - a way to enhance the embedded data shown on social networks. Something we'll add to FFConf, but for now, here's how to start:

- Mat "Wilto" Marquis has an in depth post with [Implementing Standard.site](https://wil.to/posts/standard-site/)
- If you're more of "I need charts" dev, then [Steve Simkins post](https://stevedylan.dev/posts/indexing-standard-site/) is for you
- Prefer lots of code? Then [ege's weblog](https://hypersubject.net/entries/2026/06/integrating-standard.site-with-my-hugo-blog/) has a good walk-through
- There's also tooling emerging: [Sequoia CLI](https://sequoia.pub/) and a [validator](https://site-validator.fly.dev/)
- Andy Bell also includes Wordpress and Astro plugins [in his post](https://bell.bz/i-did-the-standard-site-thing/).

Finally, next week is [Pixel Pioneers](https://pixelpioneers.co/events/bristol-2026), they still have handful of tickets, plus still space for students. Not a bad idea if you want to get some mid-year web goodies.
