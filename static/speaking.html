Conference days

The conference registration opens at 9:00am. The schedule is in this email (and will appear fully on https://ffconf.org on Thursday).

I'll introduce each of you, but please make sure you find me at the start of the break if you're on in the next segment so that I can make sure you're mic'ed up with your lapel mic.

I'm assuming you have your own laptop, if not that's cool just make sure you've emailed me your presentation (either as a PDF or Keynote or something mac friendly) by the Tuesday before the event.
